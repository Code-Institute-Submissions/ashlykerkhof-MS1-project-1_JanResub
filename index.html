About me

Click Here for my Story!

Ashly Kerkhof was born in raised in Peterborough, United Kingdom, before moving to London to live and study from the age of 20. Ashly studied at Tech Music school for a year before enlisting in to the Royal Central school of Speech and Drama, where he studied the art of acting, dancing, and acting through song.

Since Graduating Ash has been around the world performing in certain destinations such as, Maldives, Oman, Venice and his latest adventure which took him to the seas of the Caribbean.

Many of Ash’s credits include… Syd Normans Pour House (performed on Norwegian Cruise line, directed by the producer of Rock of Ages), Frankies Guys ( a Frankie Valli tribute band that toured Ireland for 4 months), but mostly known for his work as the front man in Fully Funktional, which he has been apart of for over 4 years. Ash wants to broaden his horizon and perform on West End, his dream shows are.. Rock of Ages, Bat out of Hell and Jesus Christ Superstar.

Ash has also dabbled in film and tv and is capable of performing infront of camera, check out his showreel below!
